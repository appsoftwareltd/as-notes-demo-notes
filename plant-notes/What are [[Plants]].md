# What are Plants?

Excellent question. Sit down.

A [[Plant]] is, at its core, a life form that has made the bold evolutionary decision to stay in one place and just... deal with whatever comes. No running. No hiding. Just vigorously growing, photosynthesising, and occasionally dying when you forget about it for two weeks.

## The Technical Bit

[[Plants]] belong to the kingdom *Plantae*. They are:

- **Autotrophic** — they make their own food from sunlight (see [[[[Plant]] Foods]] for supplementary nutrition)
- **Sessile** — fancy word for "stuck where you put them"
- **Remarkably patient** — they will wait a very long time for you to get your act together and water them

## The Philosophical Bit

In a deeply relatable move, plants silently communicate their distress by turning yellow ([[Yellow Leaves]]), going floppy, or — in extreme cases — simply giving up entirely. Sound familiar?

## Are Plants Conscious?

Some research suggests [[Plants]] can sense light, gravity, touch, and even certain chemicals. Whether they're *aware* in any meaningful sense is up for debate. Whether your [[Monstera]] is dramatically performing distress to manipulate you into watering it more... less up for debate.

## So Why Keep Them?

- They make oxygen (you need that)
- They make rooms look nice (you want that)
- Caring for something other than yourself builds character (allegedly)
- They're excellent at keeping secrets
